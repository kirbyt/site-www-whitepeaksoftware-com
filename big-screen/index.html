Big Screen

See your camera's live video stream on your TV

Watch Your iPhone's Video Stream on TV

Big Screen lets you display the live video stream from your iPhone or iPad's camera to any AirPlay device. Big Screen is the perfect app when you want to display the live video stream on your TV (requires Apple TV connected to your TV). Best of all, you see only the live video stream on your TV. There is no evidence of the app such as chrome surrounding the video stream.

Use Big Screen at your next conference, meeting, or social gathering to display live video on stage-side TVs and monitors, and help the folks in the back of the room see whatâ€™s happening on stage.

Big Screen can also be used as a video monitor to watch activity in another room. Just place your iPhone or iPad in the room pointing to the area you want to monitor, connect to AirPlay, and watch the fun from your TV.

Big Screen is fun for kids too. Turn on Big Screen, and let your kids have fun seeing themselves and friends live on TV.

** Big Screen requires Apple TV (or other AirPlay receiver) to display the live video stream on TV and monitors.

** Big Screen sends only the live video stream to AirPlay. Sending audio is not support.
