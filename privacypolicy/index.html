Privacy Policy

White Peak Software Inc is committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that gives you the most powerful and safe online experience. This Statement of Privacy applies to the White Peak Software Inc Web site and governs data collection and usage. By using the White Peak Software Inc website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

White Peak Software Inc collects personally identifiable information, such as your e-mail address, name, home or work address or telephone number. White Peak Software Inc also collects anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your ZIP code, age, gender, preferences, interests and favorites.

There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by White Peak Software Inc. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring Web site addresses. Note: White Peak Software Inc does not read any of your private online communications.

White Peak Software Inc encourages you to review the privacy statements of Web sites you choose to link to from White Peak Software Inc so that you can understand how those Web sites collect, use and share your information. White Peak Software Inc is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on Web sites outside of the White Peak Software Inc and White Peak Software Inc family of Web sites.

Use of your Personal Information

White Peak Software Inc collects and uses your personal information to operate the White Peak Software Inc Web site and deliver the services you have requested. White Peak Software Inc also uses your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from White Peak Software Inc and its affiliates. White Peak Software Inc may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

White Peak Software Inc does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party. White Peak Software Inc does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent.

White Peak Software Inc keeps track of the Web sites and pages our customers visit within White Peak Software Inc, in order to determine what White Peak Software Inc services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within White Peak Software Inc to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

Use of Cookies

The White Peak Software Inc Web site use "cookies" to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize White Peak Software Inc's pages, or register with White Peak Software Inc site or services, a cookie helps White Peak Software Inc to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, and so on. When you return to the same White Peak Software Inc Web site, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the White Peak Software Inc features that you customized.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the White Peak Software Inc services or Web sites you visit.

Security of your Personal Information

White Peak Software Inc secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. White Peak Software Inc secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other Web sites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.
