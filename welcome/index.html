Welcome

Let me start by saying thank you for picking me to work on your project. It's individuals like you that make it possible for me to be an independent software developer, and I'm forever grateful for your trust in me. I'm excited to work with you, and I look forward to seeing what we accomplish together.

Since we're just getting started, I'm sure you have questions. Hopefully this welcome packet answers those questions. If it doesn't, then send me an email and I'll get back you with an answer.

What to Expect

Each project is different, and I will speak to you directly about project specific details. But in general, you can expect to hear from me at least once a week, often times more, as I work on your project.

I generally work in one to two week sprints. Together we decide what needs to be accomplished in each sprint. And I'll let you know immediately if I run into any unexpected problems that could cause a delay.

Based on the terms of our arrangement, I'll send you invoices via email. You have the option to pay by check, PayPal, and credit card. A link for online payment is included in the invoice email.

My Availability to You

Generally I'm available Monday through Friday from 10 am to 5 pm Eastern. If you email me outside those times, I might not reply as quickly. Also, I only meet during business hours.

Tuesday's and Thursday's are often the best days for meetings. This allows me to focus uninterrupted on your project during the other days of the week.

Like everyone, I take vacations from time to time. And I occasionally take time off to attend or speak at conferences. I'll let know you well in advance when I plan to be away so my time off doesn't impact the project. Also, I'm unavailable on American holidays.

How to Reach Me

Email is the best way to reach me, but I'm also available in IM, text, and Slack for quick questions. I'm happy to chat over the phone, FaceTime, and Skype as long as we schedule the call beforehand. If it is urgent we talk, send me a quick IM or text message first. This gives me a chance to find a good stopping point so I can give you my full attention.

Next Steps

To get started, I need a couple of things from you.

  • Please send me your billing information including the email address I should use when sending you an invoice.
  • Please subscribe to my email list exclusive to my clients. On this list, I'll provide you with updates about our services, articles and information I've found that may be valuable, and the occasional anecdote. The emails will be infrequent, and you can unsubscribe at any time.
