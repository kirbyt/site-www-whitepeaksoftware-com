Bringing Your Vision To Life

OUR PROCESS

Our process takes your vision from concept to the App Store and beyond.
We can work with you through the entire process or you can select the
phase that you need assistance with. It's up to you.

  • Plan

    We start by creating a roadmap, which is a detail plan of what you want to accomplish. Armed with this plan of attack, your ready to build your mobile solution.

  • Build

    We can build your mobile solution for you, or work with your own development team to bring your vision to life.

  • Deploy

    We deploy frequent test versions so you can see the progress. And once the app is complete, we help you deploy and launch to your customers. This includes deploying to the App Store, Custom B2B deployment, and Enterprise distribution.

  • Support

    You're invested in your mobile solution, and so are we. That's why we offer ongoing montoring, maintenance, and support for your app.

Pricing

Most vendors provide you a cost estimate based on hours in hope of giving you a rough idea of how much the project will cost. Unfortunately most software project take longer than expected, which means you end up paying more money.

We take a different approach pricing. We provide you a quote based on the business goals you wish to achieve. The quote is the price you pay and not a penny more. And if we unable to help you achieve your goals or you're not satisfied with the work, then we will give you a full refund.
