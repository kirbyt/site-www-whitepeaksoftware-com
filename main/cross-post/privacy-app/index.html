Data Collection

Cross Post does not collection or report any personal information to our company or outside third parties. In order for Cross Post to work it must use certain third party services. We are not responsible for nor can we control any data collected by these third party services.

Third Party Services

Cross Post requests permission to access third party services using your account. You must grant permission and access to these services in order for Cross Post to update your status and upload photos to each service. Access tokens to third party services used by Cross Post are stored on your own mobile device, and the access tokens can be removed by you at any time.

Access tokens to third party services are shared with the Cross Post server to reduce the amount of time needed to post to the third party services. The Cross Post server does not permanently store your access token, and the access tokens are removed immediately after the post is sent.

Cross Post uses the following third party services:

App.net

Cross Post requires "write post" permissions to use App.net. This enables Cross Post to update your status and upload photos. Cross Post reads your App.net account information to display your user name and avatar photo within the application. Cross Post does not read other information, collect data, or do anything else with your account.

Facebook

Cross Post requires "publish_actions, user_photos" permissions to use Facebook. This enables Cross Post to update your status and upload photos. Cross Post reads your Facebook account information to display your name and profile photo within the application. Cross Post does not read other information, collect data, or do anything else with your account.

Twitter

Cross Post requires access to your Twitter account to update your status and upload photos. Cross Post reads your Twitter account information to display your user name and profile photo within the application. Cross Post does not read other information, collect data, or do anything else with your account.

Security

We take security seriously, and we have taken the necessary steps to protect your data. Access tokens to third party services are securely stored on your device in the keychain, and all communication between the app and third party services takes place using HTTPS.
