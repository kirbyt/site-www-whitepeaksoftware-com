NOT SURE WHAT IT WILL COST TO BUILD YOUR MOBILE APP?

You have a great idea for a mobile app, but you’re not a software developer. That’s fine; you can hire a someone to help bring your app idea to life. But how much will that someone cost you?

You've probably already done your homework to get a rough idea of what building an app will cost you. You've asked family members and friends who write software, or you've searched Google. Maybe you've talked with other business owners who have hired a developer before.

As you educate yourself about the cost of building a mobile app, you've learned the price can vary. One developer will quote you an estimated dollar amount while another gives you a quote that's double, triple, even higher. And as you continue your research, you find that most software projects end up costing more than expected because the developer didn't do they homework before quoting you an estimated price.

You have a great app idea, but now you're paralyzed with the fear of not knowing how much it will cost to create your app.

IMAGINE KNOWING THE COST UPFRONT

When you create a mobile app, you are creating an asset, one that hopefully makes you money. Creating the asset means making a financial investment in that asset. Now imagine knowing what your financial investment will be upfront. Imagine being able to budget the project ahead of time. Imagine never hearing the dreaded words, “I need additional hours to complete this, which is going to cost you $$$$ more,” in the final hours of the project.

BUILDING A ROADMAP TO SUCCESS

To determine your upfront financial investment, you need a clear vision of what your app will do. To achieve this, you and I will spend a day together discussing your project down to the tiniest detail. After our session, I'll spend the next couple of weeks producing a set of deliverables for you that include:

  • A complete list of user stories
  • A set of wireframes for the user interface
  • An interactive prototype based on the wireframe
  • A recommended high-level application architecture
  • A list of suggested services to support your application, from hosting providers for backend servers to technology recommendations for syncing data across devices to crash reporting and analytics

I will also provide you guidance on what to include in the MVP, your Minimum Valuable Product, which is a fancy name for the first version of your app that your customers use.

The deliverables are yours to keep. You can do what you want with them. You can even use the deliverables to hire a different software developer. If, however, you decide to continue working with me, I will provide you a fixed price quote for your project. The price quote is not an estimate. It’s the amount you pay and not a penny more.
