About Us

White Peak Software is an independent software company founded by Kirby Turner in November 2003. Our mission is to deliver quality software at an affordable price. White Peak is a full-time, solo venture for Kirby allowing him to pursue his passion for creating great software that people find helpful and enjoy using. Kirby is also an avid snowboarder, hiker, and backpacker, and enjoys spending quality time with his wife and son when not sitting behind the keyboard.

Follow White Peak Software on Twitter at @whitepeak or for a more personal perspective follow Kirby at @kirbyt.
